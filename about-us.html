About Us

What We Offer: Angel Ronnie foods is a family luxury event caterer. Our foods have a strong emphasis on authentic home cooking with carefully sourced and hand-picked ingredients from the best producers and local suppliers. All meals are well-seasoned and prepared in the most hygienic environment. We specialize in Nigerian, English, and Philippine-style menus. We specialize in bespoke event catering for weddings, small or grand dinners for private parties, and corporate functions. Our focus is on how to make sure you have a memorable experience when we serve you. Customers Are Our Top Priority We offer excellent services and quality products in all areas of our business. When you are ready to make your needs a reality, it begins with contacting our company to help you begin the process.
